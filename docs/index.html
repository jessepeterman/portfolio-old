    About Me

    I've had a passion for computers and technology ever since I could remember.

    It all started when I intuitively learned how to setup the Nintendo for my family at 2 or 3 years of age. In Jr. High I went through the grueling process of installing Linux on my PC, and that got me into the world of terminals, commands, and gave me an introduction to programming.

    I have experience with various operating systems and technologies, including open source, and I'm currently studying Javascript and Full Stack web development full-time, specializing in React and Node.JS.

    In addition to my technical background, I'm also an experienced leader with a demonstrated history of working in the religious/non-profit institutions. I'm skilled in Leadership Development, Management, Creative Problem Solving, Teaching, and Public Speaking. Strong technical skills and community/social services professional with a Master of Divinity (M.Div.) focused in General/Pastoral from Talbot School of Theology.

    I also am married to my beautiful wife of 12 years and together we have four crazy kids. They keep me plenty busy!
